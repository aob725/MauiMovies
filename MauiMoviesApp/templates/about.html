Welcome to Maui Movie Central v1.0!

Maui Movie Central was created as a fun way to log reviews amongst my friend group. At the beginning of quarantine we had a movie system where we would vote each week for what movie we wanted to watch and then rate the movies. I thought what better way than to create a local "Rotten Tomatoes" type review website.

Maui Movie Central (MMC) gets its name from Maui, a kitten my roommate and I adopted. She is a ball of energy and spends 90% of her time sprinting around, jumping, stalking, and attacking. The other 10% she spends cuddling and sleeping.

There are still many improvements to be made and I have several already in mind. Please see below and let me know if there are any features you would like to be incorporated!
